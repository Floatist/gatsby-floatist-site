Cindy has a Bachelors in International Business Administration and a Masters in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Amsterdam. She started sailing at a young age, and through university she worked as a skipper on sailing yachts, where she experienced many of the inefficiencies in the yacht charter industry first hand.    
  <br></br>

With her skills as software engineer and her 5+ years of experience as a skipper, she set out to solve the many challenges yacht charter operators face on a day to day basis.
